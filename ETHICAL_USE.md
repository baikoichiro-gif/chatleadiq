# Ethical Use

Use ChatLeadIQ only with WhatsApp accounts and conversations you are authorized to manage.

- Respect customer consent.
- Respect opt-out requests.
- Do not use for spam.
- Do not use for bulk unsolicited messaging.
- Do not use for scraping contacts.
- Do not use for deception.
- Human approval is required before sending any reply.
- Follow WhatsApp/Meta and local messaging regulations.

ChatLeadIQ is a CRM assistant, not a bulk sender or spam automation tool.
